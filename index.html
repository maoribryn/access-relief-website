    FAQs

    How can plant-based treatments help me?

    Plant-based treatments offer therapeutic benefits for various conditions. Let's explore the possibilities together during a consultation.

    What happens during a consultation?

    Our doctors will discuss your medical history, current symptoms, and treatment goals to determine if prescription medicines or other plant-based medicines are right for you.

    Are Consultations Confidential?

    Some prescription medicines may affect your ability to drive. Your doctor will discuss any restrictions and safety considerations during your consultation.

    How much does a consultation cost?

    View our pricing and book online through our scheduling page. Use code ACC for $25 off ACC consultations or code CSC for $15 off for CSC holders (valid claim or card required).

    Are consultations confidential?

    Absolutely. Your privacy is our top priority. All medical consultations and records are strictly confidential.

    How do I book an appointment?

    Simply click the "ACCESS A DOCTOR" button or "yes, call me back" to get started. You can also book directly through our online scheduling page.
